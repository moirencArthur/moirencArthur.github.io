Beer :

Guinness Draught - An iconic stout beer with a rich, creamy flavor, notes of coffee, and chocolate.

Weihenstephaner Hefeweissbier - A German wheat beer, brewed since 1040, with aromas of banana and clove, slightly spicy, and refreshing.

Sierra Nevada Pale Ale - An iconic American beer, brewed in the pale ale style, with aromatic hops and tropical fruit notes.

Chimay Bleue - A Belgian Trappist beer, brewed by the monks of the Scourmont Abbey, with complex flavors of dried fruits, caramel, and spices.

cocktail :

Margarita - A classic cocktail made with tequila, lime juice, and triple sec, often served with salt on the rim of the glass

Old Fashioned - A timeless cocktail made with whiskey (typically bourbon or rye), sugar, bitters, and a twist of citrus rind.

Mojito - A refreshing Cuban cocktail made with white rum, fresh mint leaves, lime juice, sugar, and soda water.

Cosmopolitan - A popular cocktail made with vodka, cranberry juice, triple sec, and freshly squeezed lime juice, often garnished with a twist of lemon or lime.
